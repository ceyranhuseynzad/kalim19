Navagio, Greece Chosen as the beach of the year.

Enclosed by cliffs and facing out over azure waters, Navagio Beach in Greece is so picturesque it even comes with its own shipwreck. The strip of beach is accessed only by boat. Regular tours to the beach depart from Porto Vromi to the south, and from Agios Nikolaos and Cape Skinari to the north-east. Boats also arrive from the harbour of Zakynthos city.
